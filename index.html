Ritzenthaler, T., 16th Annual European DDI User Conference (EDDI 2024)This work focuses on the automation and application of open standards to improve the processing of cross-national longitudinal panel surveys, as exemplified by the Generations & Gender Programme (GGP). How comparable are the cross-national surveys in the Generations and Gender Survey Round II?06 2024
Connolly, A., Ritzenthaler, T., Grünwald, O., Caporali, A., European Population Conference - EPC 2024International surveys implement methodologies aimed at achieving comparable data across countries. These methodologies encompass the same questionnaires across countries and guidelines about data collection operations.
